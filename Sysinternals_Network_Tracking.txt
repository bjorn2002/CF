Practical. No. 6
Aim:- Using Sysinternals tools for Network Tracking and Process


Step :-
1.Download the Sysinternals Suite

A) Check Sysinternals tools
2. Click Extract Files
3. Click on Procmon > Click On Agree.
4. Click On process Monitor Filter > Click On Reset > Add click >
Click On Apply

B) Monitor Live Processes
5. Click On tools > Click On Process tree.
6. Click On Tools > Process Activity Summary.

C) Capture RAM-Capture
7. Click On RAMMap > Click On Agree.

D) TCP/UDP packets
8. Click On tcp view > Click On Agree.

E) Monitor Hard Disk
9. Click On vmmap > Click On Agree

F) Monitor Virtual Memory
10. Click On Ok

G) Monitor Cache Memory
11. Click On Cacheset > Click On Agree.
